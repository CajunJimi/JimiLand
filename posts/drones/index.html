Drones

2024-12-30

Used AI to summarise multiple reddit posts that contained links. Formatting sucks for my eyes, but whatever. Will be trying to document as much footage/images around these ’UFO’ or ‘Drone’ sightings here as possible.

Part 1: United Kingdom Incidents (November 2024)

• Location: U.S. military bases in the UK: RAF Lakenheath, RAF Mildenhall, and RAF Feltwell.

• Events:

• Unidentified drones breached highly secure airspaces over several days.

• Countermeasures such as the Orcus counter-drone system, deployment of 60 British troops, and F-15 fighter jets were ineffective.

• The drones evaded radar, jamming, and interception—suggesting advanced technology far beyond commercial capabilities.

• Whistleblower Revelations:

• Similar incidents occurred earlier, including at Langley AFB.

• Drones displayed sophisticated maneuvers and a high degree of stealth, with no effective response mechanisms available.

• Drones would turn on lights near bases, seemingly as a taunt, and none of the deployed equipment could bring them down.

• Whistleblower testimony is documented in a Daily Mail report.

Part 2: U.S. Drone Incursions (November–December 2024)

• Initial Reports:

• Sightings began mid-November in New Jersey (NY Post), rapidly spreading across 36 states.

• Witnesses described drones as SUV-sized, with bright, pulsating lights or orb-like appearances (The Sun).

• Governor Murphy of New Jersey stated, “The drones are very sophisticated. The moment you get your eyes on them, they go dark” (NY Post).

• Technological Anomalies:

• NJ police investigations revealed the drones have no heat signatures (NewsNation).

• Reports suggest drones with zero thermal emissions, an unprecedented feat of stealth technology.

• Notable Locations Breached:

• Military installations such as Wright-Patterson AFB, Picatinny Arsenal, and Camp Pendleton.

• Nuclear sites and emergency service airspaces have also been impacted (NJ.com).

• Key Events:

• Airports like Logan International faced shutdowns due to drone activity.

• A Chinese national was arrested for flying a drone over Vandenberg Space Force Base.

• A Coast Guard witnessed a swarm of drones emerging from the ocean (NewsNation).

Part 3: Broader Global Incidents

• Additional Locations:

• Rammstein Air Base in Germany reported similar breaches.

• Nuclear facilities worldwide experienced a massive uptick in drone sightings.

• Arrests vs. Mystery:

• Local arrests for reckless drone operations (NY Post) contrast with the lack of accountability for coordinated incursions.

Patterns and Implications

• Shared Characteristics:

• Drones are large, brightly lit, and capable of advanced evasive maneuvers.

• Operate primarily at night and hover near sensitive or secure locations.

• Unanswered Questions:

• What entity or entities are responsible?

• Are these drones conducting surveillance, testing defenses, or posing an active threat?

• Why has identification or interception proven so difficult?

Conclusion

The scale, sophistication, and global spread of these drone incursions suggest a challenge to existing defense systems and raise critical questions about national and global security. Whether the work of a state actor, private entity, or an unknown force, these incidents demand urgent investigation and effective countermeasures.
